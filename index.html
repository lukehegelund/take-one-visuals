The Filmmaker

I'm Luke, the founder of Take One Visuals. Since the day I made my first short film, I've been captivated by the creative filmmaking process: writing stories, capturing beauty, bringing viewers into an experience.

In 2022, I realized that this passion could be used to tell other people's stories, so I set out with the goal of making the most beautiful wedding films in the Inland Northwest. I call them wedding films — not videos — because that's what I deliver: not a recording, but a memory; not a file, but a feeling.

If we haven't had the chance to meet yet, I would love to connect over coffee or a phone call and hear your dreams for your wedding, and hopefully help you remember them forever.

Luke Hegelund
